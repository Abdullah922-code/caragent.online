      Class:

      High-performance sports coupe

      Starting Price:

      Around $74,000, though it can climb significantly with optional features and trims.

      Engines:

      M4 Competition:

      Equipped with a 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline-6 engine producing around 503 horsepower.

      M4 CSL:

      A more extreme variant with a tuned 3.0-liter inline-6 engine delivering up to 543 horsepower.

      Comes with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission. The manual option is particularly favored by driving enthusiasts.

      The M4 Competition offers an optional xDrive all-wheel-drive system, while the standard M4 comes with rear-wheel drive. The M4 CSL features rear-wheel drive and is focused on pure driving dynamics.

      The M4 features a sophisticated suspension setup with adaptive dampers, and an electronically controlled rear differential for enhanced handling and stability. It also has performance brakes and optional carbon ceramic brakes for improved stopping power.

      Design:

      The interior of the M4 is sporty yet luxurious, with high-quality materials such as leather, Alcantara, and carbon fiber. The design includes M-specific details like unique badging and stitching.

      The M4 comes equipped with BMW's iDrive 8 system, featuring a high-resolution touchscreen, advanced navigation, and smartphone integration (Apple CarPlay and Android Auto).
